My primary school life

Amar primary school er naam kachoir primary school.
Eti savar thanar shimulia unioner kachoir grame obosthito..
amar ei school er thik pechone kachoir islamia madrasha o etimkhana oboshito. school poshchim dike eke beke chole geche metho poth.
tar ektu dur diye boye geche amar naam na jana ekti nodi...
Chotobelai abba o boro bhai der shathe ei naam ojana nodite maach dhorte jetam. oi somoy nodite chiring baila namok ek dhoroner maach powa jeto.
chiring baila ekhon r dekha jayna. somoyer shathe r songrokkhon byabostha na thakai machta hariyei gelo.

about my teachers

madam er naamta obosso mone nai. Unake amar bishon pochondo hoyechiolo. Ami take mone mone maa vabtam.
